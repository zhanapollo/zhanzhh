Dr. Zhi-Hui Zhan received the Bachelor’s degree in computer science and technology in 2007, and the Ph. D. degree in 2013 from Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU), Guangzhou, P. R. China. He was a Lecture with the School of Information Science and Techonlogy, SYSU, from 2013-2014, was an Associate Professor with the School of Advanced Computing, SYSU, from 2013-2014, and is now a Professor with the School of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT).

His current research interests include particle swarm optimization, genetic algorithms, ant colony optimization, differential evolution, and other evolutionary computation techniques and their applications. He has published over 60 papers in international journals and conference proceedings, including about 20 IEEE Transactions papers, 1 IEEE Magazine paper, 1 ACM Surveys paper, and 3 Elsevier papers (2 Information Sciences and 1 Applied Soft Computing). 1 of them is the Essential Science Indicator (ESI) Hot Paper, and 5 of them is the Essential Science Indicator (ESI) Highly Cited Paper. The “Adaptive Particle Swarm Optimization (APSO)” and the “Orthogonal Learning Particle Swarm Optimization (OLPSO)” are both listed as the Research Fronts of ESI. His published papers have been cited over 2500 times by the scholars all over the world, according to Google Scholar or Google Scholar (for Chinese).

Dr. Zhan’s doctoral dissertation was awarded the China Computer Federation (CCF) Outstanding Dissertation in 2013 and Guangdong Province Outstanding Dissertation in 2014. Dr. Zhan received the Natural Science Foundation for Distinguished Young Scientists of Guangdong Province, China and was awarded the Pearl River New Star in Science and Technology in 2014. He is the Pearl River Scholar Young Professor. Dr. Zhan is listed as one of the Elsevier Most Cited Chinese Researchers in Computer Science. He is the regular reviewer of the journals of IEEE Transactions on Evolutionary Computation, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Electronics, IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, IEEE Computational Intelligence Magazine, Information Sciences, Journal of Scheduling, Soft Computing, Natural Computing, Memetic Computing, and many other SCI international journals and Chinese journals. He is a Speical Sessions Chair of The Seventh International Conference on Information Science and Technology (ICIST 2017), the program committee (PC) member for SSCI 2017, AAAI 2017, ICSI 2016, TAAI 2015，SMCE 2015, ICSI 2015, BRCIS-CCI'2015, ICSI 2014, APSCC 2014, TAAI 2014, TAEA 2013, and CCML 2015, CCDM 2014/2011, the Special Session Organizer on “Brain Storm Optimization and Its Applications” of the 2014 IEEE World Congress on Computational Intelligence (WCCI 2014)，the Special Session Organizer on “Neural Networks: Model and Application in Real World Problems” and “Interactive Computational Intelligence for Industry 4.0” of WCCI 2016.
